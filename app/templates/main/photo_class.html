Photo Classes

What to do with all those buttons? This course helps you understand how to use the settings on a DSLR to create great images. The transition from a point and shoot or phone to high level camera is exciting and can be intimidating. The quality of image that you can get is amazing but the hundreds of settings can get in the way if you don’t know how to use them. When you finish this course you will be up and running and creating amazing photos. (1-3 hour evening session - $75.00)

The Instructor

Scott Rodgers has been active full time in photography at Stuart-Rodgers since graduating from University of California at Berkeley in the early 1980’s. Growing up in the field and being involved in the evolution of photography continues to provide challenge and an open creative outlet for him. As the technology has changed, the opportunity for creativity has changed with it. He has had work published in local and national magazines while continuing to photograph for local clients.
