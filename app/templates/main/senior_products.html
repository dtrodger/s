Retouching

It is common to require some retouching on your portraits. We offer different levels of retouching to accommodate your needs. Retouching options include:


  • Basic Retouching - includes blemishes, shine, and under eye retouching
  • Premium Retouching - includes blemishes, under eye, shine and stray hairs and/or glass glare removal
  • Braces - includes all of the above as needed or requested and brace removal. This is done in a natural way for the best results
  • Other - is by quote. This could include scar removal, thinning a face or other more complicated requests

Please contact us at 877-307-0762 with your specific request.
